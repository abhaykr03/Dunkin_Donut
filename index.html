The Whole Story

It all started in 1946 when Bill Rosenberg opened his first coffee and donut shop, the ‘Open Kettle’.

It was immensely successful and this led Bill to start another shop called Dunkin’ Donuts. He opened the first Dunkin’ Donuts in 1950 in Quincy, Massachusetts in the USA. Today, Dunkin’ Donuts can be found in 30 countries worldwide in more than 10,000 locations. It has become the world’s leadin’ Coffee and Baked Goods chain, servin’ more than 900 million donuts and 1.5 billion cups of coffee a year globally. And now it’s also in India.
